As a Medical Doctor with over a decade of experience, my journey has intricately woven through the fields of Information Technology, Data Management, AI, and Healthcare.

My foundation is built on a blend of formal education in healthcare and self-taught expertise in data and IT, empowering me to devise innovative solutions that address the unique challenges of various organizations, from small startups to large public health bodies.

Since graduating from medical school in 2013, I've traversed diverse environments, including hospitals, clinics, and tech startups, while also contributing to public health initiatives and the UN. My work has not only spanned a wide range of domains—medical, public health, data, AI, blockchain, and finance—but has also introduced and integrated IT and data culture into Myanmar's healthcare system.

Holding a Master's degree in Healthcare Informatics from the University of Wisconsin, Milwaukee, awarded in 2023, I am a fervent self-learner, constantly exploring new advancements. Presently, my focus is on harnessing AI to revolutionize healthcare and applying web3 technologies to enhance humanitarian aid efforts.

Project Showcase

Launched in January 2020, prior to the global pandemic declaration, this project utilized Google Sheets and Data Studio dashboards to deliver a critical resource. The Election Dashboard, launched in November 2020 amidst the Myanmar general election, stands out as one of the premier data dashboards for accessing near real-time results in a detailed format. Utilizing only Google Sheets and freely available tools, it skillfully applies techniques to extract, transform, and load data, paving the way for effective visualization.

MMspring emerged following the February 2021 coup in Myanmar as a dashboard designed to aggregate and visualize data related to humanitarian incidents occurring on the ground. Initially focused on self-collected data, the project later transitioned to utilizing published data from other organizations to monitor and provide insights into the evolving conflict situation.
